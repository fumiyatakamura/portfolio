About Myself

Fumiya Takamura is a front-end developer, based in Vancouver, Canada. He is finishing New Media & Web Development program at BCIT in Oct 2015.


Fumiya learned basics of software engineering and mechatronic engineering in Integrated Information course at Aoyama Gakuin University in Japan for two years. He dropped out the university because he decided to come to BCIT to focus on web development. Fumiya wanted to place himself in the fast-pacing digital media industry in North America.


Fumiya is very curious about new technologies, not only front-end technologies. He likes thinking and creating ideas about new web applications. Someday, he will start new business to bring his ideas to life.


Fumiya loves shooting video with his DSLR camera, Nikon D7000. Fumiya is a web developer, but he has a great passion for video development. Now, his skills cover shooting, sound editing and visual effects. He is still trying new techniques and technologies about video development. He creates a lot of video works as a personal project when he is not in school.
